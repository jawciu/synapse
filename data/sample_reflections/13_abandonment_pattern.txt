My dad left when I was 11. No big fight, no explanation — he just moved out one weekend while I was at a friend's house. I came home and his closet was empty. Mum said he needed space. He called less and less. I think that's where it started — this thing I do where I interpret every cancellation, every unanswered text, as the beginning of someone leaving. When Sarah cancelled plans last week, the rational part of me knew she was tired. But there's another part, a younger part, that went straight to "she's going to disappear just like dad did." I hold on too tight to people because I'm always bracing for them to vanish. I exhaust myself monitoring for signs of abandonment that probably aren't there.
